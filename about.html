What is CodeRamp? :

This is a task based competation for students organized by JHC's Ict club to improve their knowladge in the field of Ict and Designing.

How to apply CodeRamp? :

If you are in the ages between 13-18 and studying in the Jaffna District, then you can apply for it by just filling the form in our website.
